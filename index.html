How This Works:

  • This app uses your full address to find officials at federal, state, and (if available) local levels.
  • Click "Send Email" to open your default email client with the message pre-filled.
  • To use a dedicated email server, you’ll need to connect via SMTP (e.g., Gmail API, Mailgun, or SendGrid) — this requires backend integration.
